FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT FOR THE SUPPLY OF IT SERVICES

This Framework Agreement is entered into on 15 January 2025 between HSBC Bank plc (“Client”)
and ABC Technologies Ltd (“Supplier”).

1. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain valid for twenty-four (24) months.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
The Client shall pay all valid invoices within thirty (30) days of receipt.
Any delay beyond thirty days shall attract a late payment penalty of five percent (5%) per month.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Supplier shall maintain strict confidentiality of all business, customer,
and financial information received from the Client.

4. LIABILITY
The Supplier’s total liability arising from this Agreement shall not exceed €1,200,000.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days written notice.
Immediate termination is permitted in case of material breach.
