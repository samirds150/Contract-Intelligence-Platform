GOVERNMENT SUPPLY CONTRACT

This Agreement is made on 20 March 2025 between the Ministry of Finance,
Government of India (“Purchaser”), and DEF Supplies Pvt Ltd (“Supplier”).

1. CONTRACT DURATION
This Agreement shall remain valid for twelve (12) months.

2. CONTRACT PRICE
The total contract price shall not exceed INR 75,00,000 inclusive of taxes.

3. DELIVERY
Goods shall be delivered within fifteen (15) days of receipt of purchase order.
Delays shall attract a penalty of two percent (2%) per week.

4. PAYMENT TERMS
Payments shall be released within thirty (30) days of delivery and acceptance.

5. TERMINATION
The Purchaser reserves the right to terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days notice
or immediately in the event of fraud or misrepresentation.
