PUBLIC PROCUREMENT CONTRACT

This Contract is concluded on 1 February 2025 between Sievo Oy (“Contracting Authority”)
and XYZ Facility Services Pvt Ltd (“Contractor”).

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Contractor shall provide facility management and maintenance services.

2. CONTRACT VALUE
The total value of this contract shall not exceed €500,000.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Invoices shall be settled within forty-five (45) days of submission.
Payments delayed beyond sixty days may result in service suspension.

4. COMPLIANCE
The Contractor shall comply with all applicable EU labor and safety regulations.

5. TERMINATION
The Contracting Authority may terminate this Contract with sixty (60) days written notice
or immediately in the event of fraud or regulatory non-compliance.
